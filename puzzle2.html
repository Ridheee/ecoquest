AIR

"Clean air shouldn't be a privilege dictated by where you can afford to live but a right to which we are all entitled."
-Kevin de Leon
It is clear that good air quality is important, but sometimes it is unclear how to commit to an environmentally friendly lifestyle. In the US, about half the air pollution comes from cars and trucks. Fewer trips in your vehicles, regular smog check, taking public transportation can help you reduce your carbon emission. Additionally, reducing energy consumption helps reduce air pollution since power plants burn fossil fuels to generate electricity.

Your task is to solve the riddle and spot the hidden pollutant:
I am a gas that's found up high,
In the upper atmosphere I lie,
I protect you from the sun's strong rays,
And make up smog on other days.
