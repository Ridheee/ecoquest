WATER

For many of us, clean water is so plentiful and readily available that we rarely, if ever, pause to consider what life would be like without it. However more than a billion people lack adequate access to clean water. If we have to control water pollution, we will have to find out a way out, and devise laws and strategies. Treating the poisonous substances and discharge materials and toxic substances coming out of the homes and factories before discharging them, throwing garbage in dustbins only, not wasting water unjustly can be a few control measures that we can take as individuals.

Your task is to solve the riddle and spot the hidden pollutant:

I am something that you use every day,
But when I'm disposed of, I don't go away.
I'm not metal, nor paper, nor cloth,
I'm something that's light, and I'm something that's soft.
